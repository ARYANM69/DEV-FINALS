WE CAN REMOVE THE LINE IN BETWEEN ROWS OF A TABLE USING A STYLE SUCH AS:
tfoot tr { border: none; }
For other sections, use the thead and tbody selectors as needed.
MARKS SHEET
SUBJECT MARKS
MATHS 20
HINDI 20
SST 20
SCIENCE 20 
        

Let's understand what happens:
We want to remove the middle line between MATHS and SCIENCE.
So, add tfoot to SCIENCE and thead to MATHS. 
Make sure that HINDI, SST, and SUBJECT MARKS remain in their own positions.
Then, give them the same tag, which is thead.
